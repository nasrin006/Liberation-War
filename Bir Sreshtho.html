The Seven Martyrs (Bir Sreshtho)

The bravest heroes of the Liberation War 1971

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Mohiuddin Jahangir

Known for his heroic efforts in the Rayer Bazar bridge operation in Dhaka during the final days of the war.

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Mohiuddin Jahangir

Birthday: March 6, 1945

Sector-7

Martyr Day: December 9, 1971

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Hamidur Rahman

Fought valiantly at the Battle of Dhalai, near the Tripura border.

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Hamidur Rahman

Birthday: February 2, 1953

Sector-2

Martyr Day: October 8, 1971

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Mostofa Kamal

Sacrificed his life holding back Pakistani forces in an ambush near the border.

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Mostofa Kamal

Birthday: December 16, 1947

Sector-2

Martyr Day: April 18, 1971

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Matiur Rahman

Attempted to defect with a T-33 training aircraft from Pakistan to join the Liberation War.

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Matiur Rahman

Birthday: October 29, 1941

Air Force

Martyr Day: August 20, 1971

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Mohammad Ruhul Amin

Fought bravely on the warship BNS Palash during the Khulna operation.

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Mohammad Ruhul Amin

Birthday: 1935

Sector-10

Martyr Day: December 10, 1971

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Munshi Abdur Rouf

Held his position with a machine gun preventing enemy advance until he was martyred.

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Munshi Abdur Rouf

Birthday: May 1, 1943

Sector-1

Martyr Day: April 18, 1971

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Nur Mohammad Sheikh

Died covering the retreat of his comrades during an engagement with Pakistani forces.

Bir Sreshtho Shaheed Nur Mohammad Sheikh

Birthday: February 26, 1936

Sector-8

Martyr Day: September 5, 1971
